Bloop Mockup App

In winter quarter of 2015, I participated in the Cal Poly Hackathon. With just a team of designers, we created a mockup app called Bloop. This app was a community driven app to help individuals explore new, popular, and exciting parts of their town. The information was shared through different photos uploaded by users.

Since our team was lacking developers, we used Invision to help display our app during the demonstrations. Our process started with creating wireframes, delegating design responsibilities, and finally adding each screen to Invision.
