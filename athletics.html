Cal Poly Athletic Designs

I have been the lead Graphic Designer for Cal Poly Athletics for over a year. I work under the Senior Marketing Coordinator, Stacia Momburg. I have created everything from posters and schedule cards to social media pieces. Every sport at Cal Poly that needs some type of marketing or design piece goes through me.

This job is very rewarding, and as dramatically increased my Adobe Creative Suite skills. I am now much more efficient, detail oriented, and creative. The projects that I am show casing are the Baseball Poster of 2014, a website ad, a ticket to an annual event, and a softball pledge form.
