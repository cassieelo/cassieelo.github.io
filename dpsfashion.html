Easy Outfit DPS iPad Application

One assignment I was given during college was to create a functioning Digital Publishing Suite (DPS) application for an Apple iPad. I used this opportunity to create a fashion blog. The app consisted of 8 different pages (both horizontal and vertical) that had working buttons, touch and drag photos, and other various elements. This application was designed and created created through InDesign.
