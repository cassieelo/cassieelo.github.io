About Me

My name is Cassandra Carlene Logan. I am 22 years old and was born and raised in Fresno. I currently attend California Polytechnic State University, San Luis Obispo. I am majoring in Graphic Communications with a concentration in Web and Digital Media and a minor in Media Arts and Technology. I love art. I love the different forms of art, and how it allows people to express themselves how they want. I hope to continue with a career in the creative industry, rather that be with graphic design, film, or fashion. However, I am also interested in management positions, ideally still in the creative field.
