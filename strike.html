Fictitious Cosmetic Website

This project was from Graphic Communication 339, also known as Web Design and Production, and was considered my first successful attempt of web development and design. This fictitious website got the ball rolling in what I hope a very promising career path.

I created a fictitious cosmetic website because I have always enjoyed doing makeup, and it seemed like an inspiring topic. I constructed a whole personal identity for my website, and molded the website to fit her personality.
