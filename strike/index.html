Samantha Jones

I am 25 years young and was born and raised in Easton, Maryland. I graduated from NYU in Fashion Design and am now a Makeup Artist in LA. Strike was created to inspire and help others achieve some of my favorite looks at an affordable price. I want to showcase my drastic style, and open the eyes to people who never thought makeup could be art.


I created a cosmetics line called Strike that will be found in Sephoria stores near you. Please try them out and let me know what you think!
